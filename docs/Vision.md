Most people trying to lose weight focus only on the number on the scale.

However, sustainable progress is influenced by multiple factors, including nutrition, sleep, stress, energy levels, physical activity, and daily habits. Without visibility into these factors, it is difficult to understand why progress stalls or succeeds.

This application helps users build self-awareness and long-term consistency by tracking body measurements, nutrition, sleep, stress, energy levels, habits, and overall progress in a single place.

The goal is not only to help users lose weight, but to help them understand their behavior, build better habits, and create a sustainable lifestyle that supports long-term physical and mental well-being.

# Product Mission

Transform raw daily data into actionable insights that help people understand why they overeat, lose motivation, or fail to stay consistent.
